So Nintendo announced a new Console...

Nintendo, a Japanese video game company, announced the new model for the Nintendo Switch, on July 6th of 2021, more than 4 years after the launch of the original switch and nearly 2 years after the latest revision, where they increased battery life.

The announcement that took place via YouTube was out of the blue as they were hush about it during E3 and on other platforms. Nintendo tweeted every new coming feature after this announcement in a thread. The biggest difference being the new 7inch screen on the device and the new dock with a native ethernet port.
